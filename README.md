☕ Coffee Shop Sales Performance Analysis – End-to-End Business Analyst Case Study
📌 Project Overview

This project demonstrates an end-to-end Business Analysis lifecycle performed for a multi-location coffee shop chain facing stagnant revenue growth and uneven store performance.

The objective was to identify business problems, analyze sales performance, gather stakeholder requirements, and propose data-driven solutions to improve revenue and customer engagement.

🎯 Business Problem

The coffee shop chain experienced:

Declining sales after peak morning hours

Underperforming store locations

Low weekend revenue

Poor performance of specific product categories

Lack of targeted promotional strategies
